Fragment 002 — Project

Luminous
Terrain

A procedural landscape generator that creates infinite, dreamlike terrains in real-time. Built with custom GLSL shaders and rendered at 60fps. Featured at Ars Electronica 2024 and exhibited at the New Museum.

Three.js GLSL Generative
Fragment 003 — Project

The Listening
Machine

An interactive installation that translates ambient sound into evolving visual compositions. Microphone input drives particle systems, color palettes, and geometric structures in real-time. Commissioned by the Barbican Centre.

Web Audio Canvas Installation

Void
Atlas

A data visualization of deep space objects, mapping over 10,000 galaxies from the Sloan Digital Sky Survey into an explorable 3D universe. Users can navigate through cosmic filaments and discover the large-scale structure of reality itself.
